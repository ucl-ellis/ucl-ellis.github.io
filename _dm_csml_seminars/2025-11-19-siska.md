title: "Convergence of policy gradient methods for reinforcement learning in general state and action spaces"
summary: "Policy gradient methods have been used to develop efficient reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms over the last decade. This talk will review recent results on the convergence of policy-gradient-type methods for RL in general state and action spaces, focusing on the mirror descent method with and without function approximation, its connection to the natural policy gradient (NPG), and the role of convexity (or lack thereof) in the objective function."
